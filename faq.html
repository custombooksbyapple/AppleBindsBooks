About the Process

Book rebinding is the process of replacing or restoring a book's cover and/or binding structure. This can involve repairing a damaged spine, replacing worn covers, upgrading a paperback to a hardcover, or giving a beloved book an entirely new look. The interior pages are kept — only the structure around them changes.
Yes! I accept custom commission work. You would ship your book to me, I'll assess its condition and discuss your preferred style, and then we'll agree on a quote before I begin. Use the Contact page to get the conversation started.
Most books can be rebound as long as the text block (the interior pages) is in reasonable condition. Novels, poetry collections, art books, journals, Bibles, vintage hardcovers, and paperbacks are all common candidates. If you're unsure, send me a description or photos via the Contact page and I'll let you know what's possible.
Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity of the binding and my current queue. Simple rebinds can take 1–2 weeks, while more elaborate commissions with tooling, gilding, or specialty materials may take 3–5 weeks. I'll always give you an estimated timeline when we discuss your project.
I work with a range of materials including genuine leather, bonded leather, bookbinding cloth (linen, cotton, buckram), decorative papers (Italian marbled, Japanese Chiyogami, hand-printed), and archival-quality adhesives. All materials are chosen for their durability and aesthetics. I'm happy to discuss specific material preferences when planning your commission.

Ordering & Payment

Click the Inquire button on any listing and you'll be taken to the contact form with that listing pre-selected. Send me a message, and I'll follow up with payment details. I currently handle sales directly rather than through an automated checkout.
I currently accept payment via PayPal, Venmo, and bank transfer. Payment details will be shared once we've confirmed the details of your order or commission. For commissions, I typically require a 50% deposit upfront, with the remainder due before shipping.
Pricing depends on the size and condition of the book, the materials selected, and the complexity of the binding style. As a general guide, simple cloth rebinds start around $40–$80, while leather or elaborate decorative bindings can range from $100–$250+. I'll always provide a firm quote before any work begins.
Because each rebind is custom handmade work, I'm unable to offer returns in most cases. However, if your order arrives damaged or significantly different from what was agreed upon, please contact me within 7 days and I will do my best to make it right.

Shipping & Handling

Currently I ship within the United States. International shipping may be available on request — reach out via the Contact page and I can look into options and costs for your location.
Books are wrapped in acid-free tissue, then packed snugly in a rigid box to prevent movement during transit. I take great care with packaging because I know how much your book matters to you.
Once we've discussed your project and agreed on the details, I'll provide my mailing address. Please pack your book carefully and use a tracked shipping method. I'm not responsible for books lost or damaged in transit to me, so I recommend insurance for especially valuable volumes.
